About

I am Helen Doherty - I love the arts. Books are my favourite, but I love everything from theatre and film to the visual arts. I dabble in a bit of painting and do a lot of doodling - you can see some of these in the fun section.

 

I work as a Digital Producer in London and specialise in telling complex stories, often with data.

 

The rest of my time is spent chasing kittens around the house! You’ll come across photos of the three of them as you scroll down. Cats are what the internet was made for, right?

 

This website is a showcase of me - what I like and what I do (both in and out of work).
